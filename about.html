Our Story

Where Memories Are Made

Playfield Park is Port Harcourt's premier destination for family fun, corporate events, and unforgettable celebrations. Nestled in nature, we offer a perfect blend of excitement and tranquility.

Since 2016, we've been creating magical moments for thousands of visitors. From our thrilling amusement park rides to serene garden events, splash-tastic pools to cozy accommodations, we've crafted an experience that keeps families coming back year after year.
