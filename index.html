Where Memories Are Made

Playfield Park is Lagos' premier destination for family fun, corporate events, and unforgettable celebrations. Nestled in nature, we offer a perfect blend of excitement and tranquility.

From our thrilling amusement park rides to serene event gardens, aquatic fun pools to comfortable hotel accommodations, we have everything you need for the perfect getaway.
